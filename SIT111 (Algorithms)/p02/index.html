Deakin’s response to COVID-19

Deakin and its campuses are open. For now, students and staff should continue to attend as planned. Deakin continues to monitor the COVID-19 pandemic to ensure the safety of the University community. We continue to update our public information in line with advice from the State and Federal health authorities.

Latest University updates about COVID-19

Bushfire crisis support

As a community, we stand together to recognise and support those that have been impacted by this persistent state of emergency and the devastation and displacement that bushfires bring. The widespread impact of the disaster continues to grow, and Deakin is committed to supporting all students and staff affected.

Research

Research at Deakin is about changing the world. Our innovative research centres help to solve global challenges, create smarter technology, and improve the health and wellbeing of communities. With a diverse range of research areas, close links with industry, and first-class facilities, Deakin research creates real-world, far-reaching impact.

Find out more

Business and community

Deakin has a deep history of collaboration. We have forged partnerships with leaders in industry, government, and research to solve problems, share ideas, and inspire work-ready graduates. From developing community initiatives, connecting with global universities, to innovating with industry, Deakin promotes collaboration in everything we do.

We're here to help

Whether you're looking at changing degrees, need guidance applying or just want to know more about a particular course, our Future Student Advisers are here to help.

Get help with your application

We understand that applying for university can be stressful. That’s why we offer a free personalised service to support you along the way. With an Application Assist appointment, you can receive step-by-step guidance while we share a screen over Skype.
